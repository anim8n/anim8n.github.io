ANIMOON is a creative team composed of professional artists specializing in the development of visual and animation full-service solutions. The office locations are in Beijing and Singapore. The main creative team members come from schools such as the French School of Animation and the Beijing Film Academy. They have over 10 years of working experience in the industry and have participated in the development and production of many theatrical films.
The works created and produced by the studio members and collaborating artists have won nearly 30 awards and nominations at international film festivals in over 20 countries around the world, including the Short Film Screening Award at the Cannes Film Festival, the ADC Creative and Design Award in New York, and the Finacci Commission Competition Award, among others. The studio is also a registered company and long-term partner of LINE, NetEase, Alibaba and PopMart. The off-campus practical teaching base of Beijing Institute of Fashion Technology has been deeply cooperating with Beijing Film Academy.
Since its establishment, it has been providing high-quality creative animation production services for domestic and foreign clients, with business scope covering film, advertising, game promotion, etc. The internal pipeline covers 2D frame-by-frame animation, packaging film production and the entire 3D process.
