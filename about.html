About

ANIMOON is a premier creative visual and animation studio headquartered in Beijing. Founded by alumni of the renowned Gobelins, l'École de l'Image in Paris and Beijing Film Academy, ANIMOON brings together expertise from two of the world's most prestigious institutions in animation.

Our team comprises award-winning directors and artists whose work has been showcased at prominent international film festivals, including the Cannes Short Film Showcase, Annecy International Animation Film Festival, Venice Film Festival, and Feinaki Beijing Animation Week.

Our studio offers a full pipeline of services, encompassing 2D animation, Motion Graphics, and 3D production. Since inception, we have delivered high-quality animation services to both domestic and international clients, specializing in a wide range of content, from animated films and advertisements to game cinematics.

In recent years, ANIMOON has expanded its focus to developing original animated series, short films, and feature-length productions, actively exploring new opportunities for creative storytelling.
